# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 8, 2026**

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Monday.com App Web Integration ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Monday.com API Token**: Used to access your Monday.com boards (stored locally in your environment)
- **Board IDs**: To identify which boards to sync
- **Email Address**: For contact forms and support requests (via Resend API)

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: Application logs for debugging and performance monitoring
- **Error Reports**: Technical information when errors occur

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Connect to your Monday.com boards and retrieve data
- Export data to Excel templates as requested
- Send email notifications (if configured)
- Provide technical support
- Improve application functionality

## Data Storage and Security

- **API Tokens**: Stored locally in your `.env` file, never transmitted to our servers
- **Exported Data**: Saved locally in the `output/` directory on your machine
- **Email Data**: Processed through Resend API with encryption in transit
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not store your Monday.com data on external servers

## Third-Party Services

This application integrates with:
- **Monday.com API**: Subject to [Monday.com Privacy Policy](https://monday.com/l/privacy/privacy-policy/)
- **Resend API**: Subject to [Resend Privacy Policy](https://resend.com/legal/privacy-policy)
- **Vercel** (for web hosting): Subject to [Vercel Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data is only shared with:
- Monday.com (to retrieve your board data via their API)
- Resend (to send emails you request)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data at any time (it's stored locally)
- Delete your data (remove the application and `.env` file)
- Opt-out of email notifications
- Request support at info@manageonsite.com

## Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
